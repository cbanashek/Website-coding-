The Water Lily Pond

Info,when and how

The Water Lily Pond is one of Claude Monet's most famous paintings. He painted it in 1899. This painting is one of a series of paintings just like it, Monet called it Water Lilies. As well, this painting has been in the Metropolitan Museum of Art, The National Gallery since 1929.

As well, to know a little bit more about the painting here is a quote from , "The Water Lily Pond captures Monet’s view of his Japanese bridge. While Monet would paint this bridge at various points in time- it can be seen through the blurry lenses of his cataracts in Japanese Bridge– in this version, it is shown bathed in golden light, with a calming color palette of greens and pale pinks. "
