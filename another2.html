Oscar- Claude Monet was a famous French painter, born December 5th 1840 and raised in Rue Laffitte, France. He died at the age of 87 in 1926 in Giverny, France. And throughout his lifetime Claude Monet, has created many art pieces that we still see today in many museums. He has created paintings that were and still are priceless.
